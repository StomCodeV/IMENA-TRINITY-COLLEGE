About Us

Excellence in Rwanda Education

Kaarumuna Academy is a dedicated educational institution in Kanzenze, Ntarama, providing quality education from Nursery through O'Level. We believe in nurturing young minds with strong academics, values, and practical skills needed for their future success in Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a leading school in Rwanda that produces learned, responsible citizens with strong character and academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality education that develops critical thinking, moral values, and practical skills for all levels from Nursery to O'Level.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are the school fees for 2026?
Our tuition fees vary by level and boarding status. Day students pay approximately 150,000 RWF per term, while boarding students pay 250,000 RWF per term. Contact our admissions office for detailed fee structures.
Is boarding available?
Yes, we offer boarding facilities for both boys and girls. Our dormitories are modern, secure, and supervised by trained staff. Students receive three meals a day and have access to study halls.
What curriculum do you follow?
We follow the Rwanda Education Board (REB) curriculum for O-Level education (S1-S3). Our program includes all core subjects plus electives in ICT, entrepreneurship, and creative arts.
How do I apply for admission?
You can apply online through our inquiry form or visit our campus to collect application forms. Required documents include: previous academic records, birth certificate, 4 passport photos, and the application fee.
Do you offer scholarships?
Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships to exceptional students. Scholarships cover partial to full tuition depending on academic performance. We also have bursaries for students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
