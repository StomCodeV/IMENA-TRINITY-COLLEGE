To provide quality, holistic education that develops the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical potential of every student through innovative teaching methods, modern facilities, and a nurturing learning environment.

Admissions Process

Join our community of learners in 4 simple steps

1

Submit Application

Download our application form or submit online. Required documents include birth certificate, previous school reports, and passport photos.

2

Assessment & Interview

Age-appropriate assessment for class placement. Parent interview with school administration to discuss expectations and school policies.

3

Review & Acceptance

Applications reviewed within 7 working days. Acceptance letters issued to successful candidates with detailed enrollment instructions.

4

Enrollment & Orientation

Complete registration forms, fee payment, and attend orientation program for new students and parents.
