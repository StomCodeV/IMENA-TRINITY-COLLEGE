About Our School

Building a foundation for lifelong success

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Rwanda, producing globally competitive graduates who are intellectually equipped, morally upright, and ready to transform their communities through innovation and leadership.

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that develops the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical potential of every student through innovative teaching methods, modern facilities, and a nurturing learning environment.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity and Discipline
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Respect and Unity
  • Community Service
  • Lifelong Learning
